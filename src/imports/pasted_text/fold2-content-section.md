**FIGMA MAKE PROMPT — FOLD 2: WHAT THIS IS**

---

Create a full-width content section (desktop) that immediately follows the dark full-screen hero. This fold is the first breath of light — it transitions from cinematic and heavy to clean and editorial. The background is warm off-white. The mood shifts from tension to confidence.

**BACKGROUND & TRANSITION**
Background colour: #F4F3F1 (warm off-white). No photograph, no texture. The contrast with the dark Fold 1 is intentional and stark — the page opens up. Top padding should be generous (~100–120px) so the content doesn't crowd the fold boundary. The section should feel like a magazine feature opening page.

**LAYOUT**
Single column, left-aligned, max content width ~760px, centred on the page with generous side margins. The structure top to bottom is: eyebrow → headline → body copy → two agent cards (side by side) → closing line.

**AGENT CARDS — two columns, equal width, ~16px gap between them**

Each card is a contained block with a light background (#FFFFFF or very slightly warm white), soft border (1px, rgba(0,0,0,0.07)), border-radius 16px, padding ~32px. No heavy shadows — just a clean lift from the page.

**Card 1 — Philo**

- Identity mark (top of card): An abstract soft gradient orb, roughly 72×72px. Warm tones — peach at top-left (#FFB085), fading through coral (#FF8C7A) to a warm pink-white at centre. Softly blurred edges. No face, no eyes, no text inside it. It should feel like a warm ambient glow — a hint of the character, not the character itself. Position: top-left of card or centred at top.
- Tag line: "Philo — Optimise" — Figtree SemiBold or PP Neue Montreal Medium, ~15px, colour #2C2825
- Meta line: "Browser-based · Host + Guest" — Figtree Regular, 12px, letter-spacing 0.04em, colour #2C2825 at 45% opacity, uppercase
- Description: "Helps hosts shape their listing identity. Helps guests search by feel, not filter." — Figtree Regular, 14px, line height 1.6, colour #2C2825 at 65% opacity
- Spacing between elements: 8–10px

**Card 2 — Xeny**

- Identity mark (top of card): Abstract soft gradient orb, same size (~72×72px). Cooler tones — soft lavender-blue at top (#B8B0E8), fading through a muted rose-pink (#E8A0B8) to near-white at centre. Same blur treatment as Philo's orb. Cooler, quieter feeling.
- Tag line: "Xeny — Wander" — same treatment as Philo
- Meta line: "Mobile app · Host + Guest" — same treatment
- Description: "Turns local knowledge into a shareable guide. Lets guests experience a place the way a local would." — same treatment
- Spacing: same as Card 1

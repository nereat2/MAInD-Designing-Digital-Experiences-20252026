**HOW THE THREE SUB-FOLDS CONNECT**

They share one parent section container. The visual connective tissue is the progressive background darkening: off-white → deep charcoal → slightly warmer dark. The eyebrow label changes each time (THE PROBLEM / THE EVIDENCE / THE HUMAN COST) but maintains identical typographic treatment. No dividers, no horizontal rules between sub-folds — they flow continuously. The coral accent (#FF385C) on the eyebrows and the map dots is the single thread that runs through all three, keeping them unified.

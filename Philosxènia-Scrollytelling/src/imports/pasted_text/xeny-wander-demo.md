**HOW 8A AND 8B CONNECT**
8A is daylight — warm white, functional, host-facing. 8B is immersive dark — the aurora descending, the guest experience as atmosphere. The thin divider between them is the literal threshold. When the viewer crosses it, the page transforms. The Xeny eyebrow changes accent colour (from warm coral in the rest of the page to white on the dark background). The frames are larger. The copy is more evocative. The only thing that stays the same is the screen format — phone frames, same border radius, same hardware detail — which grounds the immersion in product reality.
